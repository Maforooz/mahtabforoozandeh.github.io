About me

My journey in UI/UX design started from 2021, when I enrolled
in UI/UX specialist courses offered by California Institution of
Art. I got my first career as a UI design intern in a software
engineering company back in my home country, a few months
later. Meanwhile, I extended my knowledge into programming
and front-end developing by practicing HTML, CSS, p5.js and
JavaScript.
I came to Vancouver from Iran in 2023 to continue my studies.
I’m currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in design majoring
at Interaction Design at Emily Carr University of Art + Design.
Recently, I found my passion for designing in 3D spaces and 3D
Interaction design for virtual realities, and I’m developing my
skills in these fields by taking courses in 3D design, virtual
reality development, and interaction design in 3D spaces.
